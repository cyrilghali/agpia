Other Prayers

Prayer is a living relationship with God. This collection gathers essential Coptic liturgical, sacramental, and daily spiritual prayers from the Agpeya and Gospel tradition. It guides believers through confession, communion, meals, study, and personal petitions, forming a structured rhythm of worship, repentance, gratitude, and disciplined devotion for faithful daily life.
